Business

BETA Technologies, Inc. designs, manufactures, and sells electric aircraft, advanced electric propulsion systems, and charging systems and components in the United States and internationally. It offers aircraft, such as ALIA CTOL (CX300), a conventional takeoff and landing aircraft for cargo services; ALIA VTOL (A250), a vertical takeoff and landing aircraft for cargo, logistics, medical operations, and passenger services; ALIA Defense VTOL (MV250), a military aircraft for cargo and logistics; motors; batteries for electric aircraft; and charging solutions, including charge, mini, and thermal management cubes. The company also provides ground service equipment and flight control systems. In addition, it offers training services through flight simulators and virtual reality training solutions. The company was founded in 2017 and is headquartered in South Burlington, Vermont.
